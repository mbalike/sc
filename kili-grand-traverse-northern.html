Grand Traverse (Northern)

A sweeping traverse across the mountain’s northern aspect — quiet paths, broad skies, and steady acclimatization for a refined summit bid.

8–9 days Moderate Fewer crowds

Why Choose Grand Traverse

Experience the advantages of the northern route through a refined traverse — scenic, serene, and beautifully paced.

Wide vistas Gentle profile

Fast Facts

Typically 8–9 days • Start via west • End Mweka • Tents • Best seasons: Jan–Mar, Jun–Oct.

Moir • Buffalo • School Hut Summit via Gilman’s
