From Zanzibar

The Chic Escape

Hop from turquoise shores to golden plains for a three-day fusion of island serenity and untamed adventure — the perfect Safari Chic getaway.

ZNZ → DAR Private 4×4 Insured

Day-by-Day Journey

Short, sharp, spectacular — all the essentials with a luxe finish.

Day 1
Flight + game drive • ~5 hrs total

Island to Savannah

Fly from Zanzibar to the mainland and journey through shifting landscapes into Mikumi or Nyerere. Your first game drive unfolds at golden hour — a swirl of light, dust, and grace beneath the African sky.

Game drive Camp Bastian / Selous Safari Camp / Siwandu
Safari Start
Day 2
Full day safari • Dawn to dusk (~10 hrs)

Golden Encounters

Dawn brings the rhythm of the bush to life. Chase silhouettes of lion and giraffe, savor a picnic in the wild, and — if in Nyerere — drift down the Rufiji River where hippos and kingfishers steal the scene.

Dawn game drive Boat safari (Nyerere) Golden hour
All Day
Day 3
Morning drive + flight • ~4 hrs total

Sunrise Farewell

A final dawn safari before returning to Dar for your flight back to Zanzibar. Arrive with sand on your skin, stories in your heart, and the rhythm of the wild still echoing through you.

Private transfer DAR → ZNZ
