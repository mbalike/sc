Lake Manyara

A compact, diverse park where forests meet lake — home to flamingos, elephants, and tree-climbing lions.

Explore Guide See Trips

Overview

Despite its small size, Manyara packs incredible diversity: groundwater forests, acacia woodlands, and the soda lake attracting vibrant birdlife.

Groundwater forest Flamingos Tree-climbing lions

Top Activities

  • Forest and lakeshore game drives
  • Canopy walkway (seasonal access)
  • Canoeing (water level dependent)

Where To Stay

Great as a day trip or a night stop en route to Ngorongoro/Serengeti. Charming lakeside lodges available.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Northern Tanzania
  • Special: Tree-climbing lions
  • Best for: Birders, families
  • Nearest Hub: Arusha
