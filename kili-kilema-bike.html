Kilema Bike Route

Ride selected lower segments on mountain bikes before linking to the Marangu trekking path—an adventurous twist on Kili.

5–6 days MTB segments Moderate
Kilema

Why choose Kilema Bike

If you love mountain biking, Kilema lets you pedal parts of the ascent before joining the classic walking route.

Ride & hike Unique angles

Fast facts

Start Kilema Gate • Link to Marangu • Mix of bike and hike • Seasons: Jan–Mar, Jun–Oct.

Kilema • Horombo • Kibo Summit on foot

Sample 6-Day Itinerary

Includes bike segments on the lower slopes, then transitions to trekking.

Day 1 Kilema Gate → Kilema Camp (bike)
Forest tracks
Intro ride.

Bike through forest tracks to Kilema Camp; support vehicle carries gear.

Kilema
Day 2 Kilema → Horombo (bike/hike)
Mixed terrain
Gain altitude.

Ride and push sections as terrain steepens; overnight in Horombo Hut.

Horombo
Day 3 Acclimatize at Horombo
Zebra Rocks
Active rest.

Optional acclimatization ride/hike toward Zebra Rocks, then return.

Acclimatize
Day 4 Horombo → Kibo (hike)
High camp
Across saddle.

Transition fully to trekking across the alpine desert to Kibo Hut.

Kibo
Day 5 Summit → Horombo (hike)
Uhuru Peak
Via Gilman’s.

Summit push on foot, then descend to Horombo for the night.

Summit
Day 6 Horombo → Marangu Gate
Exit
Celebrate.

Final descent to the gate, bike transfer back to Arusha/Moshi.
