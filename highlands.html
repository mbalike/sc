Ngorongoro Highlands

A scenic highland region of volcanic craters, cool air, and meaningful community encounters — a tranquil contrast to the savannah.

Explore Guide See Trips

Overview

Hike Empakaai’s rim with views over the emerald crater lake, or wander Olmoti’s meadows. The Highlands reward slower travel and cultural immersion.

Empakaai & Olmoti Community visits Rift Valley vistas

Top Activities

  • Guided crater rim hikes
  • Community and cultural experiences
  • Birding and scenic photography

Where To Stay

Atmospheric lodges and camps tucked into the highlands, often paired with Ngorongoro Crater or Serengeti.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Northern Tanzania
  • Vibe: Cool, serene, cultural
  • Best for: Hikers, slow travel
  • Nearest Hub: Karatu
