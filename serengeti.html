Serengeti National Park

Endless savannahs, thundering hooves, and predators on the prowl — the Serengeti is the stage for Earth’s greatest wildlife drama.

Explore Guide See Trips

Overview

The Serengeti spans 30,000 km² of pristine wilderness. Each year, over two million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles circle the ecosystem following the rains — the legendary Great Migration.

Big Five Predator action Balloon safaris

Top Activities

  • Dawn and dusk game drives with expert guides
  • Sunrise hot air balloon flight with champagne bush breakfast
  • Photo safaris focused on big cats and migration crossings
  • Visit Olduvai Gorge Museum (en route to Ngorongoro)

Where To Stay

Choose from mobile tented camps that follow the herds or luxe permanent lodges with pools and spas. We’ll match your season and style.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Northern Tanzania
  • Famous for: Great Migration, big cats
  • Best for: Wildlife lovers, photographers
  • Nearest Hub: Arusha / Kilimanjaro Airport
Plan My Safari

Insider Tips

  • Crossings are unpredictable: 2–3 nights in the north (Jul–Oct) increase your chances.
  • Calving season (Jan–Mar) offers dramatic predator interactions and incredible photography.
  • Bring a bean bag or monopod for steady long-lens shots.
