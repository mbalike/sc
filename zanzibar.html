Spice Isles

Zanzibar

A dreamy island escape of sugar-white sands, historic Stone Town, and spice-scented breezes.

Explore Guide See Trips

Overview

Zanzibar blends Indian Ocean bliss with Swahili culture. Choose lively north beaches (Nungwi/Kendwa), kite-ready east (Paje/Jambiani), or serene south-west coves.

Turquoise lagoons Stone Town Spice farms

Top Activities

  • Dhow sunset cruises and sandbank picnics
  • Stone Town walking tour and Forodhani night market
  • Snorkeling in Mnemba Atoll or Chumbe Island

Where To Stay

Boutique resorts, chic villas, and barefoot luxury lodges. We’ll tailor to your vibe — romantic, family, or friends’ escape.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Indian Ocean, off Tanzania
  • Vibe: Tropical, historic, laid-back
  • Best for: Beach lovers, honeymooners
  • Nearest Hub: ZNZ Airport
