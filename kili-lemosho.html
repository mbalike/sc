Kilimanjaro

Lemosho Route

A beautiful west-side approach crossing the Shira Plateau and joining the Southern Circuit. Excellent acclimatization, varied scenery, and strong summit success over 7–8 days.

7–8 days Moderate Great acclimatization Jan–Mar • Jun–Oct
Lemosho

Why choose Lemosho

Early days through quiet rainforest lead to the expansive Shira Plateau. The profile allows “walk high, sleep low” opportunities for better acclimatization and vistas across Kibo’s south face.

Lush rainforest Shira Plateau views Higher success rates

Fast facts

Distance ~70 km • Start: Lemosho Gate • End: Mweka Gate • Overnights: tents • Best months: Jan–Mar, Jun–Oct.

Shira • Barranco • Karanga • Barafu Gradual ascent

Sample 8-Day Itinerary

Flexible pacing available (7 or 8 days). Final plan adapts to your dates and group preferences.

Day 1 Lemosho Gate → Mti Mkubwa
Forest ascent
Drive to trailhead, hike to Big Tree Camp (~2–3 hrs).

Briefing and gear check in Moshi/Arusha, then transfer to Lemosho Gate. A gentle trek through montane forest leads to Mti Mkubwa Camp.

Safety briefing Misty forest
Mti Mkubwa
Day 2 Mti Mkubwa → Shira 1
Onto the Plateau
Emerge above the trees, enter heath and moorland.

Trail climbs steadily to the rim of the Shira Plateau with sweeping views. Camp near Shira 1 under wide skies.

Shira
Day 3 Shira 1 → Shira 2
Acclimatize
Shorter day across the plateau; optional acclimatization walk.

Traverse to Shira 2 Camp. Afternoon hike higher and descend to sleep low.

Shira 2
Day 4 Shira 2 → Lava Tower → Barranco
Walk high, sleep low
Reach ~4,600 m at Lava Tower then descend to Barranco.

A textbook acclimatization day: gain altitude to Lava Tower before dropping to the lush Barranco Valley.

Lava Tower
Day 5 Barranco → Karanga
Barranco Wall
A fun scramble up the wall; undulating traverse to Karanga.

Short, rewarding day tackling the iconic Barranco Wall with superb views of the glaciers.

Barranco Wall
Day 6 Karanga → Barafu
High camp
Reach Barafu (~4,673 m); early dinner and rest.

Prepare gear, brief for summit push.

Barafu
Day 7 Summit: Uhuru Peak → Mweka
Summit night
Midnight start to Stella Point/ Uhuru Peak, descend to Mweka Camp.

Celebrate at the Roof of Africa (5,895 m) before a long descent to lower elevations.

Uhuru Peak
Day 8 Mweka → Gate
Exit
Gentle forest descent to Mweka Gate; transfer.

Receive certificates and return to the lodge for a well-earned rest.

Mweka
